Privacy Policy

AURA is built on the belief that privacy is a right, not a feature you pay extra for.

  • Your data is stored exclusively on Canadian servers governed by Canadian law (PIPEDA).
  • We do not sell, share, or profile your data to any third party, ever.
  • End-to-end encryption is applied by default on all personal communications.
  • You can request a full export or deletion of all your data at any time.
  • No advertising networks have access to your AURA activity or profile.

Full privacy policy available at aurame.ca/legal/privacy — last updated May 2026.

  • AURA cannot use your data to train AI models without explicit opt-in consent.
  • Law enforcement requests require a valid Canadian court order — we fight for you.
